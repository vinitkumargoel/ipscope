Comment & Community Policy

Guidelines for feedback and communication with IPScope.

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Purpose

This policy governs how users may communicate with IPScope through contact forms, email, GitHub issues, and any future community features.

Expected conduct

  • Be respectful and constructive.
  • Provide accurate information when reporting bugs or data errors.
  • Do not post personal data of third parties (doxxing).
  • Do not use contact channels for spam, threats, or harassment.

Prohibited content

  • Hate speech, threats, or harassment.
  • Spam, scams, or unsolicited advertising.
  • Illegal content or instructions for illegal activity.
  • Malware links or phishing attempts.
  • False or misleading claims intended to harm others.

Moderation

We reserve the right to ignore, delete, or block communications that violate this policy. Serious violations may be reported to appropriate authorities.

Data correction requests

If geolocation data appears wrong, include the IP address, what you expected, and evidence if available. We cannot always correct third-party database entries but will investigate.

Abuse reports

To report network abuse related to an IP address, use the abuse contact shown in lookup results (RDAP abuse email) or our contact page for service-related issues.
