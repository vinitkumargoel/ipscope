Cookie Policy

How IPScope uses cookies and browser storage.

Last updated: July 6, 2026

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored by your browser. Local storage is similar browser storage used for preferences and recent activity.

What we use

  • ipscope-cookie-consent (local storage) — Records that you accepted the cookie notice. Essential for compliance display.
  • ipscope-theme (local storage) — Saves your light/dark theme preference.
  • ipscope-recent (local storage) — Stores your recent IP lookups on this device only.

Third-party cookies

We load fonts from Google Fonts and map tiles from OpenStreetMap/Leaflet. These providers may set their own cookies or collect connection data under their policies. We do not use advertising or analytics trackers by default.

Managing cookies

You can clear local storage and cookies in your browser settings. Clearing storage will reset theme, recent lookups, and consent banner.
