Privacy Policy

How IPScope collects, uses, and protects information.

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Overview

IPScope ("we", "our", "the service") provides IP geolocation lookup tools. This policy explains what information is processed when you visit our website or use our API.

Information we process

  • IP addresses: When you use "What is my IP", we read your connection IP from the HTTP request to perform geolocation. When you look up another IP, we process the address you submit.
  • Technical data: Standard server logs may include timestamps, request paths, user-agent strings, and referrer headers.
  • Browser data: On the homepage, optional client-side scripts may read browser timezone, screen size, and network type to display "Your browser" tiles. This data stays in your browser unless you export it.
  • Local storage: We store recent lookups and theme preference in your browser's local storage if you use those features.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not require account registration for basic lookups.
  • Geolocation is resolved using offline MMDB databases on our server — we do not send your IP to third-party geolocation APIs for the core lookup.

Legal bases (GDPR)

Where GDPR applies, we process IP addresses and technical logs based on legitimate interest in operating and securing the service, and based on your consent where cookies or non-essential storage are used. See our Cookie Policy.

Data retention

Server access logs are retained only as long as needed for security and operations, typically 30–90 days unless a longer period is required by law. Local storage data remains on your device until you clear it.

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of personal data. Contact us at legal@vinitk.dev to exercise these rights.

Children

IPScope is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date above.

Contact

Questions about privacy: legal@vinitk.dev or our contact page.
